Topical Wound Treatment

Topical wound treatments for pets are medicines used to clean wounds, prevent infections, promote healing, reduce pain, and protect the injured area. They are safe, easy to apply, and come in sprays, creams, or gels.

View our topical wound treatments for pets below:

ALSUPRAY

Sizes:75 g

Uses/Indications: Completely covers the wound & amp; can be applied without damaging the wound tissue Contains a proprietary carrier that helps the protective formula to adhere to the skin, so it stays where it is needed Allows the wound to oxygenate (breathe) which is essential for good wound healing, forms a protective barrier Against micro-organisms, dirt, flies, other insects & for teat ends in dry cows only Post castration Post dehorning post operative care

Dosage: A superficial application on the wounds once or twice daily. For optimal result wounds should be cleaned, disinfected & sutured if necessary before the application. or As directed by Veterinarian

Compositions:

  • Neomycin sulphate I.P. Eq. to Neomycin 3400 unit
  • Polymyxin B sulphate USP Eq. to Polymyxin 5000 unit
  • Bacitracin zinc I.P. Eq. to Bacitracin 400 unit

NEGASUNT

Sizes: 40g Bottle

Uses/Indications: Acts as a remedy for wounds of cattle, sheep, horse, goat, camel & dog.

Dosage: Shake bottle well and spray in and around the infected area.

Compositions:Each gram contains:

  • Propoxur – 20mg
  • Coumaphos – 30 mg

Sizes: 150 ml

Uses/Indications: All types of traumatic surgical wounds, maggot or worm infested wounds, pyoderma and dermatomycosis

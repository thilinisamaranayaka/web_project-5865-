Our Profile

Tails Waggers Care

Transcending the unique position through our wide portfolio of products and services

Founded in 1982 as a division of Animals Care, we are a key sector of Tails Waggers Care Holdings Limited, dedicated to enhancing the health and productivity of livestock, poultry, and domestic animals across the island. Our product range features globally renowned brands like Elanco, Hipra, Cargill-Provimi, and Vetoquinol, along with a comprehensive selection of pet care solutions. By specializing in livestock nutrition, veterinary medicine, and vaccines, we continue to promote sustainable and professional farming practices. Our Tails Waggers Care's Laboratory in Embilipitiya provides advanced diagnostic services, including feed analysis, poultry postmortems, bacteriology, water quality testing, and PCR diagnostics for livestock diseases, utilizing state-of-the-art technology. Committed to ethical and sustainable solutions, we strive to improve animal health and productivity while delivering value to farmers and consumers.

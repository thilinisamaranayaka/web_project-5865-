VACCINE

Protect birds against infectious diseases by selecting the recommended vaccination programs
Vaccination plays an important role in the health management of poultry by improving performance and profitability. Tails Waggers care offers vaccines with proven efficacy from leading global veterinary brands which prevent diseases by triggering or boosting the immune system to fight against the causal organisms.

View our Poultry Vaccines below:

AVISAN MULTI

Sizes: 1000 Doses (500ml bottle)

Uses/Indications: Inactivated vaccine for egg drop syndrome, Newcastle disease and infectious bronchitis.

Dosage: Injectable emulsion Layer 0.5 ml per bird, recommended to vaccinate 2 weeks before laying.

Compositions: Each dose (0.5ml) contains; Infectious bronchitis virus, inactivated, strain H52/ Newcastle disease virus, inactivated, strain LaSota/ Egg drop syndrome virus, inactivated, strain 127.

De Wormer

De-wormers are medications used to treat and prevent parasitic worm infestations in pets, such as dogs and cats. These worms can infect various parts of a pet's body, most commonly the gastrointestinal tract, and may include roundworms, tapeworms, hookworms, whipworms, and others. De-wormers are crucial for maintaining the health of pets, as worm infestations can lead to severe health issues if left untreated.

Complete disinfection protocol for complete hygiene

Ensure the highest level of protection against infectious poultry diseases with Tails Waggers Care Disinfectant Solutions with proven efficacy. Our Disinfectant solutions can be used on all walls, surfaces, floors, rooms, and transport vehicles.

KENO COX

Sizes:10L

Uses/Indications: Keno™cox is a broad spectrum disinfectant effective against endoparasites, bacteria and viruses. Keno™cox helps to reduce infection pressure in animal houses. It can be applied on all kinds of surfaces. Keno™cox is a unique formula (patent pending) without phenol safe for users and animals.It’s advised to use:
Calf huts, calf pens, calving boxes, and poultry houses.

Dosage:Cleaning and Disinfecting Procedure: Use an alkaline cleaner such as Biogel or Biocid-S, followed by disinfection with Virocid®, Kickstart, or Cid 20, Drying: Allow the cleaned and disinfected surfaces to dry thoroughly,Application of Kenocox Solution: Spray a 4% solution of Kenocox abundantly on the floor until it is wet (0.4 L/m2).
Pay special attention to feeders, drinkers, and all surfaces that may come in contact with animals, including damaged or worn floors or walls.
Contact Time: Allow a minimum contact time of 2 hours. Pay special attention to feeders, drinkers, and all surfaces that may come in contact with animals, including damaged or worn floors or walls.

VIROCID

Sizes:10L

Uses/Indications: Can be used for spraying, foaming, fogging without adding special agents or enhancers. Due to its sophisticated and continuously improved formulation, Virocid® can be used at extremely low dilutions. Virocid is very flexible. It can be used in: Animal husbandry (poultry, pigs, cattle, …) Hatching egg disinfection. Food processing, Horticulture, Aquaculture, Hospitals, Personal Hygiene

Dosage:First clean the surfaces thoroughly, Disinfect , Contact time: 15 minutes , Apply Virocid® at 0.25 – 0.5% (1:400 – 1:200) by spraying or foaming (allow 1L of solution per 4 m2 or 1gal per 160 ft2 ), For fogging use 1 – 2 L in 4 L water for 1000 m3 or 1 – 2 gal + 4 gal water per 130,000 ft3 ), Foot dips: 0,5-1%, renew solution regularly, Wheel dips: 0,5-1%, renew solution regularly, Truck spray: 0,25-1%, Hatching: egg disinfection: 20% (25 ml solution / m3 or 0.0066 gal per 35.3147 ft3 )

Compositions: 2 different quaternary ammonia (1 single chain: alkyldimethylbenzylammoniumchloride and 1 twin chain: didecyldimethylammoniumchloride, thus broadening the spectrum of the quaternary ammonium group), Glutaraldehyde, Isopropanol, Pine oil
